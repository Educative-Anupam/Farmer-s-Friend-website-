Agricultural Diseases

In agriculture, disease management is the practice of minimising disease in crops to increase quantity of harvest yield. Organisms that cause infectious disease in crops include fungi, oomycetes, bacteria,viruses, viroids, virus-like organisms, phytoplasmas, protozoa, nematodes and parasic plants.

Agricultural Research

Agricultural research can be broadly defined as any research activity aimed at improving productivity and quality of crops by their genetic improvement, better plant protection, irrigation, storage methods, farm mechanization, efficient marketing and a better management of resources.
